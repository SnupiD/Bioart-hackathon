3D printing material[edit]

Build volume: 25×21×21 cm (9.84"×8.3"×8.3")

Layer height: 0.05 - 0.35 mm

Nozzle: 0.4mm default, wide range of other diameters/nozzles supported

Filament diameter: 1.75 mm

  • 250g ASA filament red
  • 250g ASA filament white
  • 125g 98A Flexfilament (hard rubber)
  • 125g 40D Flexfilament (elastic, flexible and rubber-like material)
